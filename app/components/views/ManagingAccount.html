How Can I Create Account?

You can create account on Duqhan By using email address or facebook. We recommend using one of these Methods for a more personalized shopping experience

How Do I Reset My Password ?

To change your password Please follow this step . Tap the menu at the top , left hand side of the screen tap my password then enter your old password and new password then click on button

What If I Forgot My Password ?

Reset your password , please follow this steps . Tap signin and forgot password to reset it

What Should I Do If I Think My Account Was Hacked ?

If you think your account was hacked , please contact your Customer support team

How Do I Delete My Account?

If your account is registered through facebook you can disable Duqhan facebook privileges . Open your facebook app - under the more icon select setting - account setting - select apps -select logged in with facebook -select Duqhan and remove app
